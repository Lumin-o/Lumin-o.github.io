Why it's good for schools to teach foreign language's

It can help the person in the future

Learning a foreign language might be hard at first, but the struggles learning the language will better you in the future. Knowing another language can help you get a job, for example you can be a translator for a lawfirm.

It can help you make friends

When you go into college it is most likely that you won't know everybody there and not everybody is going to be speaking the same language. Knowing more than one language can help you make new friendships and with those new friendships come new opportunities.

That is why it is good to learn a foreign language
